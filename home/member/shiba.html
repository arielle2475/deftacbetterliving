Coach Martin Toledo | Purple Belt

  • ​Co-founder and Head Instructor of Deftac Betterliving.
  • ​Director and founder of the Deftac Betterliving

Coach Martin Toledo was born and raised in the heart of the Cebu, Philippines. His dream was to be a positive influence for others and help people through Jiu-Jitsu everywhere he could. Currently, he spends much of his time with his family in Paranaque where he actively teaches and oversees Deftac Betterliving’s academic program as well as all matters related to his worldwide network of Jiu-Jitsu academies known as the Ribeiro Jiu-Jitsu Association. From time to time he travels to different Ribeiro Jiu-Jitsu affiliated schools around the world where he teaches seminars and instructors’ courses.
