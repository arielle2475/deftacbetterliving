Coach Rommel Alejo | Purple Belt

  • ​Co-founder and Head Instructor of Deftac Betterliving.

Originally from Nyack, New York, Professor Jeremy Bursky moved to Manila, Philippines as a Blue Belt in 2008. Shortly after, he began his tutelage under the BAMF bothers at the BAMF MMA Center. As a very dedicated student of the art, Rommel rose quickly through the ranks receiving his Black Belt from both Saulo and Richard Lasprilla in 2013. Rommel firmly believes that Jiu-Jitsu can be used as a vehicle for personal growth, and it can help people come closer to achieving their true potential. He is known for his composure, technical prowess and positive outlook on life. Rommel currently resides in Paranaque with his lovely wife, Luciana who is also a passionate practitioner of the art. ​
